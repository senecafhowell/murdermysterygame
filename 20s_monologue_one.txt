Welcome, Detective, to the current decade- the 2020s. Full of social media, technology, 
and that one virus that shall not be named, the 2020s had a lot going for it.
You have been transported into this decade to solve a very important case.
In the very modern house that stands before you, a murder took place the previous night.
The case has yet to be solved, so the culprit is still on the loose.
It is up to you, Detective, to figure out what happened and catch this murderer before it is too late.
